Below are a few foundations that we support!

Donate now!


World Wildlife Fund

The World Wildlife Fund is a foundation designed to raise money to protect wildlife in the Amazon Rainforest. This is done by attempting to reduce the deforestation in the area. Help out by donating below!











Rainforest Action Network

The Rainforest Action Network is one of the best charities to help the wildlife in the Rainforest survive. This charity preserves forests and protects against deforestation and pollution. If you want to make a contribution to the environment and help make the world a better place, donate below!











Amazon Conservation Team

The Amazon Conservation Team works very closely with indigenous peoples that live in the Amazon Rainforest. By donating to this organization, you will be helping to save the indigenous people whose territory is being destroyed by the government. Help save these people!
