Overview of the Amazon Rainforest:

The Problem:


The Amazon Rainforest is the largest rainforest in the world. It is home to around 3000 different species of vertabrate animals, as well as 80,000 different plant species. Most of the Amazon Rainforest is located in Brazil, where the government has prioritized the economy over this forest.

The rainforest is home to countless natural resources that are of great use to the countries in which it lies; the main country being Brazil. These resources have the capability of generatiing massive wealth for these countries, and have a massive impact on their economies.
